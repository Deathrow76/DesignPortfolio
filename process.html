USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN IS THAT PART OF MY IDENTITY WHERE I ACTIVELY ENGAGE EACH OF MY INTUITIVE, ANALYTICAL, CRITICAL AND CREATIVE MINDSETS.


For me, it's not about right-brain, left-brain thinking. It's about a goal-directed design approach to the projects I work on — grounding myself in understanding, gaining perspective, embracing the collaborative process, finding ways to test multiple ideas and iterating — to arrive at the most effective and practicable solutions. The end user is on my mind through out process.
While no two projects are alike, the way I think about and approach user experience design is rather straightforward, and can be summarized below, with examples of what I produce.
